What Herons Eat

Click Here to See
Usually a heron's main food source is fish. They can eat a variety of fish including but not limited to, breams, mullet, tilapia, and carp. On occasion they also eat, insects, rodents, and even small reptiles. Herons live near bodies of water, so their food plate consists of many types of sea life (they can live off of freshwater or saltwater species).
