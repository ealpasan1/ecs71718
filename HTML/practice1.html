Temple Grandin

The Autistic Animal Lover

Temple Grandin is an autistic autism spokesperson, and an American professor of animal psychology and behavior.
She fought for the humane treatment of animals.
She also is an advocate for the autistic community.

  • Goodreads Choice Awards for Best Nonfiction
  • Award for Who's Who of Food and Beverage in America
  • Obtained a doctorate in 1989
  • Proposed better conditions for the treatment of animals
  • Featured on many TV shows, appeared in magazines, and wrote her own books
